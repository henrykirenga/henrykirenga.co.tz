Frequently Asked Questions

What types of commissions do you accept?

I accept a wide range of commissions including portraits, landscapes, abstract pieces, and custom sizes. If you have a specific idea in mind, feel free to reach out and we can discuss it further.

How long does a commission typically take to complete?

The time required depends on the size and complexity of the piece, as well as my current workload. Most commissions take between 1-2 weeks from approval of the initial concept to completion. Larger or more complex pieces may require additional time.

What is your pricing structure for commissions?

Commission prices vary based on size, complexity, and materials used. My standard sizes start at TZS 160,000 for small works (80x60cm) and go up to TZS 650,000 for large pieces (100x100cm). I'm happy to provide a personalized quote based on your specific requirements.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes, I ship worldwide. Shipping costs will be calculated based on your location and the size of the artwork. All pieces are carefully packaged to ensure they arrive in perfect condition. Tanzanian customers can also arrange for local pickup in Dar es Salaam.

Can I visit your studio?

Absolutely! I welcome visitors to our studio by appointment. This gives us a chance to discuss your ideas in person and you can see works in progress. Please contact me to schedule a visit during my available hours.

What payment methods do you accept?

I accept bank transfers, mobile payments (M-Pesa, Airtel Money), and Worldremit for international clients. A 50% deposit is required to begin work on commissions, with the balance due upon completion before shipping.
