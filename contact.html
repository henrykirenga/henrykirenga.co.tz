Good to Know

Frequently Asked

What commissions do you accept?+

Portraits, landscapes, abstract pieces, and custom sizes. If you have a specific idea, reach out and we'll discuss it.

How long does a commission take?+

Most take 1–2 weeks from concept approval to completion, depending on size and complexity. Larger pieces may need more time.

What is your pricing?+

Prices vary by size and complexity. Standard sizes start at TZS 160,000 (80×60cm) up to TZS 650,000 (100×100cm). I'm happy to provide a personalized quote.

Do you ship internationally?+

Yes, worldwide. Shipping is calculated by location and size, with careful packaging. Local pickup is available in Dar es Salaam.

What payment methods do you accept?+

Bank transfer, mobile payments (M-Pesa, Airtel Money), and WorldRemit for international clients. A 50% deposit begins the work.
