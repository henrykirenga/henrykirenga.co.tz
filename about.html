My Artistic Vision

Art is my language of freedom.

My work is rooted in freedom. I don't confine myself to a single subject or style I paint portraits, animals, nature, emotions, and anything that sparks inspiration. Whether it's a quiet expression on someone's face or the wild energy of a charging bull, every painting is a chance to explore new ideas and connect on a deeper level.

"Each stroke breaks boundaries, each color tells a story untold by words."

I mainly use acrylic, working with both brushes and palette knives the knife being my favorite tool for building rich texture and movement. I'm especially inspired by my environment, from the earthy textures of coffee seeds to the colorful patterns of African fabrics.

Philosophy

Art for all.

I choose to keep my art affordable and accessible, especially for my local community not because I value it less, but because I believe art should be for everyone, not just a few. For international collectors, that means supporting work rooted in purpose and integrity. For locals, it means making space for connection through creativity.
